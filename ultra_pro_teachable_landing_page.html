Frequently asked questions

Common questions before starting your first online course or digital product.

Do I need to be an expert?

No. You need to be able to help a specific person get a specific result. Many successful offers teach beginner-friendly skills in a clear, simple way.

What can I sell with Teachable?

You can use Teachable to sell online courses, coaching, digital downloads, and membership-style offers.

Do I need a big audience?

No. A focused offer can start with a small audience, content traffic, email, ads, partnerships, or direct outreach.

What should my first course be about?

Start with a problem you have already solved for yourself or others. The best first offer is specific, practical, and easy to understand.

How fast can I launch?

You can start simple with a small course, template pack, coaching offer, or digital download, then expand as you learn what your audience wants.
